I am preparing to up my game in different projects to create a steady stream of revenue to supplement my salary.

This means I have to get serious about this stuff and not quit if the numbers aren't there and work harder.

As such I have switched back to Fantastical on my iOS devices and am making use of spreadsheets in Google Sheets, and for my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCWi0HlYe3si17IRc-dauGGg), I've signed up for some Analytics tools and [Epidemic Sound](https://www.epidemicsound.com/) which is what most creators use.

My home screens reflect this change.
