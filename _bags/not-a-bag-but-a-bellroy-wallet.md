*Chase Reeves is a bad influence on me*.

And as such, I decided to go with a slimmer wallet, the [Bellroy Leather Slim Sleeve Wallet, Tangelo](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07NZWRWHC/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o02_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1).

This color is wacky and weird for a wallet which is why I like it. So I bought it. After two days of use[^1], here are my thoughts.

### Build

Not going to find a better build slim sleeve wallet. Seriously. Buy one and see.

### Value for Price
<a class="fasc-button fasc-size-medium fasc-type-flat fasc-rounded-medium" style="background-color: #f5a105; color: #ffffff;">Good</a>

Easily the best *made* wallet I own. My $15 [Clifton Heritage Leather Wallet](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07SVQS6B9/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o03_s02?ie=UTF8&psc=1) is bifold so there is a lot of organization, and it is also has RFID protection and is much bigger for *much less*.

At first I was really disappointed with the Bellroy wallet. It may be too small for me. I may keep it or I may go with the [Bellroy Low Wallet](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07FSQPF5Q/ref=twister_B07GVPWYKN?_encoding=UTF8&psc=1) which is also slim but a bit larger and with a bit more organization.

My disappointment does not detract from how great this wallet is. It's spectacular. I just wish I could have seen it up close and personal.
